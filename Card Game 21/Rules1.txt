1. 1-5 cards are allowed.
2. If satisfied with the added-up number of cards you “Stay”
3. If dissatisfied with the added-up number of cards you click “Hit me”
4. If number passes 21 you go bust and lost automatically (the AI wins)
5. Face cards (e.g. King, Queen, etc.) count as 10 points
6. At the beginning you receive 2 cards and the dealer gets 2 but one is face side down
7. Aces can count as 1 or 11 points
